Mean Opinion Score (MOS)

Voice Conversion

比馬特

Deep Learning and Media System Laboratory, CSIE

NCU Taiwan

This webpage offers to evaluate subjectively the results of voice conversion as measured by human hearing.

There are two types of measurement:

  • Naturalness : Measures the human-soundingness of synthesized speech (not a robotic voice)
  • Speaker similarity : Measures the similarity of the voice to the conversion target depending on the voice's characteristics (this similarity is not related to the sentence that the utterance pair spoke)

We randomly assigned three methods (A, B, and C) to avoid tagging and ensure fairness. Each method is consists of two baselines and one proposed methods.
